üìä Conversion Tracking (CRITICAL!)

Without conversion tracking, you're flying blind. This tells Google when someone submits your form so it can optimize.

‚ö†Ô∏è THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT STEP

If you skip this, Google won't know which ads are working and will waste your money. Conversion tracking is NON-NEGOTIABLE.

7.1: Create Conversion Action in Google Ads

Setup Steps

Step 1: In Google Ads, click "Tools & Settings" (wrench icon, top right)
Step 2: Under "Measurement", click "Conversions"
Step 3: Click "+ New Conversion Action"
Step 4: Select "Website"
Step 5: Enter your website: https://tradeflow.blog
Step 6: Click "Scan" (Google will try to find your form)
Step 7: Select "Add a conversion action manually"

7.2: Conversion Action Settings

8.2: Week 1 - Learning Phase

What to Expect (Days 1-7)

  • Impressions: Your ads will start showing immediately
  • Clicks: Expect 5-20 clicks per day per campaign
  • Conversions: First leads may take 2-7 days
  • Google Learning: Algorithms are learning who to show ads to
  • Don't panic: High CPC and low conversions are normal in week 1

‚ö†Ô∏è DO NOT Make Changes in Week 1

Resist the urge to pause keywords or change bids! Google needs 7-14 days to learn. Making changes too early resets the learning phase.

8.3: Week 2-4 - Optimization

After 1 week of data, start optimizing:

Weekly Optimization Tasks

Review Search Terms: Keywords ‚Üí Search Terms ‚Üí Add irrelevant terms as negatives
Pause Low Performers: Keywords with 0 conversions after 50 clicks
Boost Winners: Increase bids 20% on keywords with 3+ conversions
Test New Ads: Add 2-3 new ad variations per week
Check Quality Score: Keywords ‚Üí Columns ‚Üí Quality Score (aim for 7+)

8.4: Key Metrics to Monitor

Metric Target Action If Below Target
CTR (Click-Through Rate) > 3% Improve ad copy, test more headlines
Quality Score 7-10 Improve landing page relevance, ad-keyword match
Conversion Rate > 5% Optimize landing page, test form placement
Cost Per Conversion < ‚Ç¨30 Pause expensive keywords, add more negatives
Avg. Position 1-3 Increase bids, improve Quality Score

8.5: Month 2+ - Scale What Works

‚úÖ Scaling Strategy

After 30 days, you'll know what works. Scale like this:

  • Spain outperforming Italy? Increase Spain budget to ‚Ç¨1,000/month, keep Italy at ‚Ç¨500
  • Certain keywords crushing it? Create separate ad groups for top performers
  • Getting cheap conversions? Double your budget every 2 weeks
  • CPA under ‚Ç¨15? You have a winner - scale aggressively!

8.6: Advanced Tactics (Month 2+)

üöÄ Take It to the Next Level

  • Remarketing: Show ads to people who visited but didn't convert
  • Similar Audiences: Target people similar to your converters
  • Ad Schedule: Identify best days/hours and bid more during peak times
  • Device Optimization: If mobile converts better, increase mobile bids by 20-50%
  • Location Refinement: Identify top cities and create dedicated campaigns
  • Expand to Display: Add display campaigns for broader reach
